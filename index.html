Who can use your services?

Anyone aged 18 and above can use our platform including tertiary institution students.

 Is your platform secure and private?

Yes. We utilize industry-standard encryption and adhere to data protection regulations to ensure the security of your personal and health data.

How do I book an online consultation with a doctor?

Simply log in to your account, choose the “Consult a Doctor” option, select your preferred specialty and doctor, and schedule an appointment.

Can I get a prescription from an online consultation?

Yes. If medically necessary, our doctors will issue a digital prescription after your consultation.

How does home sample collection work?

You book a test online, choose a convenient time, and a certified phlebotomist will visit your location to collect samples.

What is a Healthcare Savings Account (HSA)?

Our HSA is a digital wallet where you can save money specifically for healthcare expenses, send funds to loved ones, and pay for services within our platform.
