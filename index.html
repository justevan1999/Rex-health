Rex-Health is Here

With the Rex Health App, users can seamlessly schedule appointments, purchase healthcare products, access sexual education resources, and manage their healthcare savings – all in one convenient mobile application. Join us in our journey towards a healthier Nigeria. Download the Rex Health App today!

Seamless Care,
Anywhere

Users can schedule appointments with medical practitioners of their choice conveniently through the app, with options for message, audio, video, or in-person consultations.

Laboratory Services

Rex Health is proud to offer peace of mind for you and your family at any point in time. Virtual Book laboratory tests right from your home. Receive digital results and Doctor’s consultation at speed of light.

Healthcare Savings Account (HSA)

The app features an HSA, allowing users to save, spend, and receive funds
for their healthcare needs, ensuring financial preparedness for medical emergencies.
